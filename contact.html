Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about consultations and contact

How much does a consultation cost?

Consultation fees vary depending on the complexity of your case. Please contact us for current pricing information.

How long does a consultation typically last?

Initial consultations typically last 30-60 minutes, depending on the complexity of your immigration case.

What documents should I bring to my consultation?

Bring any immigration documents you have, including passports, visas, green cards, and any correspondence from immigration authorities.

Do you offer consultations in Spanish?

Yes, Attorney Maria Colon provides consultations in both English and Spanish for your convenience.

Can I schedule a consultation outside of business hours?

While our office hours are Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM EST, we do answer calls 24/7 for urgent matters and can arrange consultations outside regular hours when necessary.

Is my consultation confidential?

Yes, all consultations are completely confidential. Attorney-client privilege applies to all communications with our office.
